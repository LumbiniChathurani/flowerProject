Red Rose

The red rose is one of the most iconic and beloved flowers, known for its timeless beauty and symbolic significance. It belongs to the genus Rosa and is a member of the Rosaceae family. Renowned for its striking color and captivating fragrance, the red rose holds a special place in the realm of flowers.

Begonia

Begonia flowers are a diverse and beautiful group of plants known for their vibrant colors and attractive foliage. They belong to the family Begoniaceae and are native to tropical and subtropical regions around the world. With over 1,800 species, begonias display a wide range of forms, colors, and sizes, making them a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.

Lotus

The lotus flower, scientifically known as Nelumbo nucifera, is an exquisite aquatic plant that holds profound cultural and symbolic significance across various societies. This enchanting flower is renowned for its unique beauty, captivating fragrance, and remarkable adaptability.

Blue Lotus

Blue lotus, scientifically known as Nymphaea caerulea, is a stunning aquatic plant that holds great cultural and historical significance. Native to various parts of Africa, including Egypt, Sudan, and Ethiopia, the blue lotus is renowned for its beautiful blue or violet flowers and its profound cultural and spiritual symbolism.

Daisy

Daisy flowers are charming and delicate blooms that belong to the family Asteraceae. They are known for their cheerful appearance and are widely recognized for their classic white petals surrounding a bright yellow center, although they can also be found in various other colors such as pink, purple, and yellow. Daisies are popular garden flowers and are often used in floral arrangements, bouquets, and decorations.
